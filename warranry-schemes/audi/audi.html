Warranty Schemes Audi

Warranty coverage, conditions and circumstances:

  1. For new genuine Audi cars, you will enjoy a 3-year unlimited number of Kilometers warranty.
  2. For used vehicles under 3 years, the warranty period is 6 months or 10,000 km.
  3. For vehicles that have been used for less than 5 years, the warranty period is 3 wins or 5,000 km.
  4. The warranty period will be counted from the time the vehicle is delivered to the customer and the customer is issued a Warranty and Maintenance Book and a "Certificate of quality inspection of used vehicles".

Note: the scope, conditions and other cases above are not covered by the warranty policy
