Warranty Schemes Suzuki

Warranty coverage, conditions and circumstances:

  1. Vehicle must be sold, completed and inspected by Authorized Dealers of Vietnam Suzuki Co., Ltd. Warranty card must be filled out in detail and sent to Vietnam Suzuki Co., Ltd. within 7 days after handing the vehicle over to the first owner.
  2. The vehicle must be operated in accordance with the instructions in the Vehicle Owner's Manual and must be periodically inspected and maintained by an Authorized Dealer of Vietnam Suzuki Co., Ltd. in accordance with the full instructions in this manual. Vehicle Owner's Manual.
  3. Warranty period for SUZUKI cars is 36 months from the date of delivery to the first owner (warranty registration date) or 100,000km, whichever comes first.

Note: the scope, conditions and other cases above are not covered by the warranty policy
